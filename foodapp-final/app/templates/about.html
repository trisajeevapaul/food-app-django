About Little Lemon

A family-owned Mediterranean restaurant bringing traditional recipes with a modern twist to Chicago

Our Story

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon owners Mario and Adrian

Our Values

What makes Little Lemon special is our commitment to quality, authenticity, and exceptional dining experiences.

Family Tradition

Every recipe comes from generations of family cooking, passed down through Italian traditions and perfected over time.

Fresh Ingredients

We source the finest Mediterranean ingredients, ensuring every dish is made with the freshest and highest quality components.

Cultural Fusion

Our menu celebrates the rich diversity of Mediterranean cuisine, blending Italian, Greek, and Turkish flavors harmoniously.

Meet the Owners

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy. Adrian handles all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.

"Our dream was to bring the authentic taste of the Mediterranean to Chicago, creating a place where families can enjoy traditional recipes in a warm, welcoming atmosphere."

- Mario & Adrian, Founders
